Project Idea

Our product aims to use a camera and possibly other sensors to detect drivers' fatigue and focus level and give warnings to the drivers if there are signs of drowsiness and/or distraction.

More than 27% of truck crashes are attributed to distraction and fatigue according to FMCSA. The goal of our project is to help prevent truck accidents from happening due to these reasons.

Materials
  • Camera
  • GPS
  • Gyrometer
